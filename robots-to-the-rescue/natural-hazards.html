A natural hazard is an extreme event that occurs naturally. It can cause harm to humans and can result in billions of dollars in damage to property or other things we care about. Natural hazards are caused by different processes.
Process
an event (or series of events) that can shape and change Earth.
Processes that can cause natural hazards fall into three categories, depending on how and where they happen.
