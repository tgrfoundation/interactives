Bioinformatician
A bioinformatician combines research in biology, medicine and health-related studies with information technology to interpret data.
Population Geneticist
A population geneticist studies inherited genetic markers to either understand their origin or how those markers would impact the different organisms they are in.
Taxonomist
A taxonomist is a biologist who specializes in the classification of organisms into related groups based on their structure, genetics and behavior.
Anthropologist
An anthropologist studies the origin, development and behavior of humans.
Ecologist
An ecologist studies the relationship between living organisms and their environment, including how humans affect other living organisms.
Biotechnology Laboratory Technician
A lab technician works in laboratories aiding scientists and researchers in their studies of living organisms.
